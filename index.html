What is cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a digital currency created by using encryption algorithms. Cryptocurrency is not controlled by any government or central authority and exists digitally or virtually. Cryptocurrency uses cryptography to secure transactions. Some popular cryptocurrencies are Bitcoin, Ethereum, Tether, and Solana. Cryptocurrency is used as an alternative form of payment.

What is cryptocurrency Airdrop?

A cryptocurrency airdrop is when a blockchain project sends free tokens or coins to the crypto community. It is a strategy used by blockchain startups to distribute tokens or coins to specific wallet addresses. The aim of an airdrop is to spread awareness about the cryptocurrency project and to encourage more people to participate in it when it becomes available.
